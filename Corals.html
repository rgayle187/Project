Corals

Removal of bio waste is an essential part of any working aquarium. This can be achaieved by Mechanical filtration, chemical filtration and biological filtration

SPS

The Small Polyped Stony corals (SPS) have small polyps on a calcareouss skeleton which can be either branching or plated.

LPS

The Large Polyped corals are generally larger calcareous corals than SPS coral and usually have a much larger fleshy polyp.

NPS

nonphotosynthetic corals lack zooxanthellae so they must obtain all of their own food by filter feeding.
